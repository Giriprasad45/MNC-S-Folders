COGNIZANT

cognizant

Cognizant Technology Solutions Corporation is an American multinational information technology services and consulting company. It is headquartered in Teaneck, New Jersey, U.S. Cognizant is part of the NASDAQ-100 and trades under the symbol CTSH. Here are some key points about Cognizant:
1.Founding and History: Cognizant was established in 1994 in Chennai, India, as Dun & Bradstreet Satyam Software (DBSS). It began as a 76:24 joint venture between Dun & Bradstreet and Satyam Computer Services. Initially, it operated as Dun & Bradstreet’s in-house technology unit, focusing on implementing large-scale IT projects for Dun & Bradstreet businesses. In 1996, Cognizant started serving external clients beyond Dun & Bradstreet. In 1997, DBSS renamed itself Cognizant Technology Solutions. In 1998, Cognizant became a public subsidiary of IMS Health after a corporate restructuring. The company conducted an initial public offering (IPO) in the same year, raising $34 million. Kumar Mahadeva was named CEO in March 1998. Over time, Cognizant expanded its services to include consulting, technology, and outsourcing.
2.Current Status: As of 2022, Cognizant’s revenue stands at US$19.42 billion, with an operating income of US$3.01 billion and net income of US$2.29 billion. The company has 355,300 employees worldwide1.
3.Services: Cognizant provides a wide range of services, including information technology consulting, outsourcing, and digital transformation. Its services span various industries, such as financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, and retail/logistics2.
4.Leadership: Ravi Kumar Singisetti has been the CEO of Cognizant since January 2023, succeeding Brian Humphries

Microsoft

Software Products: Microsoft is best known for its Windows line of operating systems, which have been widely used on personal computers. Other notable software products include:
Microsoft 365: A suite of productivity applications (such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook) combined with cloud services.
Edge: A web browser developed by Microsoft.
Visual Studio: An integrated development environment (IDE) for software development.
Mission and Vision: Microsoft’s mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. The company aims to create innovative solutions that enhance productivity, connectivity, and collaboration across various domains

TCS

tcs

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is one of the largest multinational IT service and consulting companies. It is headquartered in Mumbai, India, but has offices globally. TCS is well known in the e-governance, banking and financial services, telecommunications, education and healthcare markets.
TCS is a major player in the IT space. It currently has over 600,000 employees and a market cap of over $200 billion.
Tata Consultancy Services was formed in 1968 as one of the first IT companies in India. Many of its early customers were national banks. Its history with the large enterprise financial sector from the early days of computers provided much experience in the financial services market.
In 2004, TCS became a publicly traded company. It has since become one of the largest and most profitable companies in India.
TCS is a subsidiary of Tata Sons, a major Indian holding company. Tata Sons owns shares in many large Indian companies. Some of the industries of these other companies include airlines, automotive, consumer goods, hotels and steel manufacturing.
